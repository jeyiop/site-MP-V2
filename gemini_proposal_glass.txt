Here's the complete JSX for your premium "Nos savoir-faire" section, incorporating glassmorphism, Framer Motion animations, Tailwind CSS, and Next.js Image component as requested.

This section features a 2x2 grid of cards. Each card has a full background image. On hover, a frosted glass panel (containing the icon, title, and a short description) slides upwards, revealing a longer description that was previously hidden beneath it. The animations are designed to feel premium and sophisticated.
